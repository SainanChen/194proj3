Conclusion

In this project, we explored face morphing by producing a dynamic morphing process between 2 faces and finding mean face for a population. The most important tools are Delaunay Triangulation and Affine Transformation which allow us to transform various face features respectively and accurately. It's fun to use face morphing to adapt features from other faces and exaggerating some features!
